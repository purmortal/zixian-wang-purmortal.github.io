# Dr Zixian Wang

**Astrophysicist | Stellar spectroscopy | Galactic archaeology | Integral-field spectroscopy**

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Physics & Astronomy at the University of Utah. My research focuses on stellar spectroscopy, stellar populations, Galactic archaeology, and connecting individual-star studies of the Milky Way with stellar population studies of external galaxies.

## Research Interests

- Stellar spectroscopy and stellar populations
- Galactic archaeology and galaxy evolution
- Globular clusters and the Milky Way bulge
- Integral-field spectroscopy with MUSE
- Machine learning for stellar parameter and abundance inference
- Forward modelling and stellar population modelling
